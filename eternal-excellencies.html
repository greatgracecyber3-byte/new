Who We Are

About the Ministry

Our Story

Born Out of a Burden for Women

Eternal Excellencies Ladies Ministry was born from a deep prophetic burden — a calling to see African women step into the fullness of who God created them to be. Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, we began as a small prayer circle and have grown into a global movement touching lives across Africa and beyond.

We believe that when a woman discovers her true identity in Christ, she becomes unstoppable — in her home, her community, her nation. Every gathering, every teaching, every prayer is anchored in this conviction.

What We Stand For

  • ✓ Spiritual growth through the living Word of God
  • ✓ Women empowerment rooted in kingdom identity
  • ✓ Prayer and revival as a lifestyle, not an event
  • ✓ Excellence in every sphere of a woman's life
  • ✓ Kingdom impact that transforms communities

Our Mission

To Raise Women of Divine Excellence

Our mission is to create intentional spaces — conferences, retreats, prayer summits, and teachings — where women encounter the living God and are transformed from the inside out.

Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we equip women with the tools, truth, and community they need to walk in divine authority, spiritual maturity, and unwavering excellence.

J

Dr. Jane Mwikali

Vision Leader & Founder

A prophetic voice, teacher, and revivalist, Dr. Jane Mwikali has dedicated her life to raising a generation of women who walk in kingdom authority. With decades of ministry experience across Africa, she carries a mandate to see women arise into their divine calling.
